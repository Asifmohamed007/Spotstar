SPOTSTAR-SAMANTHA!!!


Samantha Akkineni is a celebrated Indian actress known for her versatile performances in Telugu and Tamil cinema.
Rising to fame with her debut in "Ye Maaya Chesave" (2010), she's won hearts with her roles in hits like "Eega," "Theri," and "Rangasthalam".
Beyond acting, Samantha is active in philanthropy and entrepreneurship, making her a multifaceted star.
