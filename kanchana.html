KANCHANA !!!


"Kanchana 3" is a Tamil horror-comedy film directed by Raghava Lawrence.
It's the fourth installment in the "Muni" series and features Lawrence himself along with Oviya, Vedhika, and Nikki Tamboli.
Released in April 2019, it combines horror, comedy, and drama, revolving around a man possessed by a transgender ghost.
Despite mixed reviews, it was a box office success, becoming one of the highest-grossing Tamil films of 2019.
