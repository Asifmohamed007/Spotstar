ROMEO JULIET !!!


"Romeo Juliet is a 2015 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film written and directed by Lakshman in his directorial debut.
The film stars Jayam Ravi and Hansika Motwani, with music composed by D. Imman.
The title alludes to William Shakespeare play Romeo and Juliet though the film not based on it. The film was released on 12 June 2015.
The film was remade in Odia as Just Mohabatt in 2017.
