Telugu cinema, often referred to as Tollywood, is the film industry based in the Indian states of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.
It's one of the largest and most prominent film industries in India, producing a vast number of films every year in the Telugu language.
Telugu cinema has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century and has witnessed significant evolution in terms of storytelling, technology, and global recognition.
