S. Janaki, often referred to as Janaki amma, is a legendary playback singer in Indian cinema,
primarily known for her work in South Indian languages such as Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Malayalam.
With a career spanning several decades, she has recorded over 48,000 songs in various languages, making her one of the most prolific playback singers in the history of Indian cinema.
