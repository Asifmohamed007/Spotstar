LLB: Life Line of Bachelors is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language drama thriller film written and directed by A.M Sidhique.
The film was produced by Mujeeb Randathani under the banner of Randathani Films.
The film stars Sreenath Bhasi, Vishak Nair, Aswanth Lal, and Anoop Menon in lead roles.
The cinematography was handled Faisal Ali, and Athul Vijay took charge of the editing.
