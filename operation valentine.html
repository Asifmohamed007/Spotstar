OPERATION VALENTINE!!!


Operation Valentine is a 2024 Indian action thriller film based on the 2019 Pulwama terrorist Attack and the retaliatory Balakot Air Strikes of 2019
by the Indian Air Force directed by Shakti Pratap Singh Hada, in his directorial debut, and produced by Sony Pictures and Sandeep Mudda, with co-production by God Bless Entertainment and Nandkumar Abbineni.
